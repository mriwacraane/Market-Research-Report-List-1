<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Architectural Insulating Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Low-E Insulating Glass Units</li><li>Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Architectural Insulating Glass market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Conventional Insulating Glass Units** feature two or more glass panes separated by airspace, enhancing thermal performance. </p><p>2. **Low-E Insulating Glass Units** have a microscopically thin coating that reflects infrared energy, improving energy efficiency and reducing heat loss. </p><p>3. **Traditional Reflective Insulating Glass Units** use a reflective coating to minimize solar heat gain and glare, making them ideal for commercial applications. Each type serves distinct energy efficiency needs and aesthetic preferences in architectural design.</p></p>
