<p><p>The stainless steel drainage market features various types of floor drains designed for efficient wastewater management. Traditional water seal floor drains utilize a water barrier to prevent foul odors. Spring-type floor drains incorporate a spring mechanism for easy cleaning and maintenance. Suction stone floor drains utilize suction principles for enhanced drainage. Gravity floor drains rely on gravitational force for efficient flow. Other types encompass specialty drains used in unique applications, ensuring a wide range of solutions for different drainage needs.</p></p>
