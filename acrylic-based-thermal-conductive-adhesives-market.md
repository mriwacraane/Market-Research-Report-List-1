<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic-based thermal conductive adhesives are essential for various applications due to their thermal management properties. The market can be segmented into isotropic and anisotropic types. Isotropic adhesives exhibit uniform thermal conductivity in all directions, making them ideal for general bonding applications. In contrast, anisotropic adhesives conduct heat primarily in one direction, offering better performance in specific electronic components. Both types cater to industries like electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, ensuring efficient thermal dissipation for enhanced product performance.</p></p>
