<p><strong>The Manual Directional Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-way</li><li>4-way</li><li>5-way</li><li>6-way</li><li>Multi-way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual directional control valves market is categorized based on the configuration of the valves. 2-way valves control flow in one direction, enabling or blocking flow. 4-way valves manage two separate flow paths, often used in double-acting cylinders. 5-way valves offer versatility for complex applications, directing flow across multiple outputs. 6-way valves provide additional routing options for more intricate systems. Multi-way valves further expand on this, accommodating various configurations and facilitating multiple flow paths for enhanced control in hydraulic and pneumatic systems.</p></p>
