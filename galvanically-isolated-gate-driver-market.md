<p><p>The galvanically isolated gate driver market primarily consists of IGBT drivers and MOSFET drivers. IGBT drivers are designed to efficiently control insulated gate bipolar transistors, suitable for high-voltage and high-current applications, such as inverters and motor drives. On the other hand, MOSFET drivers cater to metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, offering rapid switching capabilities for lower voltage applications. Both types ensure electrical isolation, enhancing safety and performance in diverse industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics applications by preventing ground loop issues and improving noise immunity.</p></p>
