Low Power Relays handle currents from 1A to 5A, suitable for consumer electronics. Medium Power Relays range from 5A to 10A, often used in industrial equipment. High Power Relays exceed 10A, catering to heavy machinery and automotive applications, where robust performance and reliability are crucial for effective operation.</p></p>
