<p><strong>The Night Vision Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Filtration</li><li>Laminated Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision filters enhance visibility in low-light conditions and are categorized into two main types: absorption filtration and laminated filtration. Absorption filtration utilizes materials that selectively absorb specific wavelengths of light, improving contrast and clarity at night. In contrast, laminated filtration incorporates multiple layers of specialized materials to reduce glare and enhance the transmission of desired wavelengths, allowing for clearer images in various environments. Both types cater to specific applications, such as military, security, and recreational uses.</p></p>
