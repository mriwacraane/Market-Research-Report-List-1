<p><p>The gasket sealant market primarily comprises liquid sealants and anaerobic sealants. Liquid sealants are used for creating flexible, resilient seals that can fill gaps and contours, making them ideal for various applications like automotive and industrial settings. Anaerobic sealants, on the other hand, cure in the absence of air, forming strong bonds that are effective in high-temperature and high-pressure environments. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring leak-proof seals in machinery, plumbing, and automotive components.</p></p>
