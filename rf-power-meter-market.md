<p><strong>The RF Power Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Type Power Meter</li><li>Through Type Power Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power meter market primarily consists of two types: absorption type and through type power meters. Absorption type power meters measure the power by absorbing the RF signal within a load, providing accurate readings directly related to the energy absorbed. In contrast, through type power meters determine power levels by allowing the RF signal to pass through a coupler, offering non-intrusive measurements. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including telecommunications and broadcasting, ensuring efficient signal management.</p></p>
