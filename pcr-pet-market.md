<p><strong>The PCR PET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>m-PCR(Mechanical PCR)</li><li>c-PCR(Chemical PCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR PET market consists of two main types: mechanical PCR (m-PCR) and chemical PCR (c-PCR). m-PCR employs mechanical processes to enhance the physical properties of PET, improving its processing and recyclability, while c-PCR utilizes chemical methods to break down PET into its monomers for repolymerization, yielding higher-quality recycled PET. Both types aim to innovate PET recycling and processing, addressing environmental concerns and enhancing material sustainability in various applications, including packaging and textiles.</p></p>
