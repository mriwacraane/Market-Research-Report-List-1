<p><strong>The Borehole Seismic Survey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offset Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Zero-Offset Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Deviated-well Vertical Seismic Profile</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Borehole seismic surveys include several types tailored to different exploration needs. The Offset Vertical Seismic Profile (OVSP) measures seismic response from a distance to assess subsurface structures. Zero-Offset Vertical Seismic Profile (ZVSP) collects data directly above the borehole for detailed imaging of surrounding rock formations. Deviated-well Vertical Seismic Profile targets angled wells to acquire seismic information in complex geological settings. Other methods may encompass various specialized techniques to enhance subsurface understanding and improve resource exploration efficiency.</p></p>
