<p><p>The Smart Tire Safety Monitor market is segmented into two main types: Interior Monitors and External Monitors. Interior Monitors are integrated within the tire, providing real-time data on pressure and temperature, enhancing safety and performance. External Monitors, on the other hand, are positioned outside the tire, often using sensors to relay information wirelessly to a central system. Both types aim to improve vehicle safety, optimize tire longevity, and enhance overall driving experience by ensuring optimal tire conditions.</p></p>
