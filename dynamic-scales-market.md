<p><ul><li>Single-Idler</li><li>Two-Idler</li><li>Multi-Idler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic scales are used in markets to measure weight dynamically as products move. In a single-idler market, one idler supports the scale, making it suitable for simpler applications. A two-idler market incorporates a second idler for more stability and accuracy, ideal for larger items. The multi-idler market utilizes several idlers, providing high precision and efficiency, making it perfect for complex, high-throughput environments. Each type caters to varying needs for accuracy, stability, and operational scale in weight measurement.</p></p>
