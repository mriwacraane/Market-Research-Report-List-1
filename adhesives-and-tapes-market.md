<p><p>The adhesives and tapes market includes various types based on application methods and curing processes. **Pressure-sensitive adhesives** (PSAs) adhere instantly upon light pressure, used in labels, tapes, and stickers. **Hot-melt adhesives** are thermoplastic materials applied in molten form, solidifying upon cooling, ideal for packaging and woodworking. **Light-cured adhesives** harden when exposed to UV or visible light, offering precision in electronics and medical devices. **Other types** include water-based, solvent-based, and reactive adhesives, catering to diverse industrial needs like construction, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
