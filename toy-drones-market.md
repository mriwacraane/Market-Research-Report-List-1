<p><p>The toy drones market is categorized into three main types: Fixed Wing, Rotor, and Flapping Wing. Fixed Wing drones feature a traditional aircraft design, offering longer flight times and greater stability but require more space for takeoff and landing. Rotor-type drones, commonly known as quadcopters, are versatile and easy to maneuver, making them popular among beginners. Flapping Wing drones mimic bird flight, providing unique aerial capabilities and agility, though they are less common and typically more complex in design and operation.</p></p>
