Wave-induced motion SUBs utilize gentle, wave-like agitation to promote cell growth while minimizing shear stress. Stirred SUBs employ mechanical stirring for effective mixing and oxygen transfer, suitable for various applications. Other types encompass innovative designs and technologies that cater to specific bioprocessing needs. These variations enhance flexibility, reduce contamination risks, and lower operational costs in biopharmaceutical production.</p></p>
