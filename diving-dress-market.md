<p><ul><li>Wet Dress</li><li>Dry Dress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving dress market encompasses two primary types: wet dress and dry dress. Wet dress is designed to allow water to enter the suit, providing thermal insulation through a layer of water that warms up with the body's heat, ideal for warmer waters. In contrast, dry dress maintains a sealed environment, keeping divers completely dry, suitable for colder conditions or extended dives. Each type serves specific needs, balancing comfort, thermal protection, and versatility in diverse underwater environments.</p></p>
