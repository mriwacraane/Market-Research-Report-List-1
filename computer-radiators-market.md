<p><p>The computer radiators market features several types, including air-cooled, heat pipe, water-cooled radiators, and others. Air-cooled radiators utilize fans to dissipate heat from components, offering a cost-effective solution. Heat pipe radiators employ thermal conduction through sealed pipes to enhance cooling efficiency. Water-cooled radiators circulate liquid coolant to transfer heat away from critical areas, providing superior cooling. Other types may include phase-change or gel-based systems, catering to specialized applications. Each type serves unique cooling needs in computing environments.</p></p>
