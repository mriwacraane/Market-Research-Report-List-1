<p><p>The steel cord for radial tires market is categorized into four main types based on tensile strength: Normal Tensile (NT), High Tensile (HT), Super Tensile (ST), and Ultra Tensile (UT). NT steel cord offers basic strength for general applications, while HT provides enhanced durability and resistance to deformation. ST further increases tensile strength for high-performance tires, and UT offers the highest tensile strength, suitable for extreme conditions and heavy loads, ensuring superior performance and safety in tire applications.</p></p>
