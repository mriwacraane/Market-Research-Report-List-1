<p><p>The Terrazzo Tile market comprises primarily two types: Inorganic Terrazzo Tile and Epoxy Terrazzo Tile. Inorganic Terrazzo Tile is traditionally made using cement and aggregates, offering durability and a classic aesthetic suitable for heavy-footfall areas. In contrast, Epoxy Terrazzo Tile utilizes resin-based materials, allowing for vibrant colors and intricate designs while providing excellent flexibility and moisture resistance. Both types cater to diverse architectural needs, enhancing the visual appeal and functionality of floors in commercial and residential spaces.</p></p>
