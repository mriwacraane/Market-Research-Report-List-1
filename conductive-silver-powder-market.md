<p><p>The conductive silver powder market primarily includes two types: flake silver powder and spherical silver powder. Flake silver powder features a flat, leaf-like structure, enhancing surface area and electrical conductivity, making it suitable for applications in electronics and conductive inks. Spherical silver powder, on the other hand, consists of round particles that offer uniformity and better flow properties, ideal for manufacturing applications such as paste and coating solutions. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
