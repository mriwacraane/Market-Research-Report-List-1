<p><p>Remote clinical trials can be categorized into three main market types: </p><p>1. **Interventional Trials** involve actively testing new treatments or drugs on participants to assess their efficacy and safety, with remote monitoring and data collection enhancing patient engagement. </p><p>2. **Observational Trials** focus on gathering data without intervention, allowing researchers to monitor participants' health outcomes in a natural setting remotely. </p><p>3. **Expanded Access Trials** provide patients outside of clinical trials with access to experimental treatments, facilitating remote consultations and follow-ups for potential participants.</p></p>
