<p><ul><li>Extruded</li><li>Knitted/Woven</li><li>Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Biaxial Geogrid market comprises three primary types: Extruded, Knitted/Woven, and Bonded. Extruded geogrids are manufactured by extruding polymer sheets, creating a robust grid structure ideal for soil stabilization and reinforcement. Knitted/Woven geogrids are made by interlacing fibers, offering flexibility and high tensile strength, suitable for road construction and erosion control. Bonded geogrids involve bonding multiple layers, providing enhanced durability and load distribution, often used in heavy-duty applications like retaining walls and embankments. Each type caters to specific engineering needs, ensuring optimal performance in geotechnical projects.</p></p>
