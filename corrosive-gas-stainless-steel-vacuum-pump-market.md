<p><p>The corrosive gas stainless steel vacuum pump market includes two primary types: gas capture pumps and gas transfer pumps. Gas capture pumps are designed to capture and contain corrosive gases, preventing environmental contamination and ensuring safe handling. Gas transfer pumps, on the other hand, facilitate the movement of corrosive gases through a system, maintaining efficiency while minimizing the risk of corrosion. Both types are essential in industries requiring safe management of corrosive gases, ensuring operational integrity and safety.</p></p>
