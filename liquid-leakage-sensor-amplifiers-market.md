<p><p>The Liquid Leakage Sensor Amplifiers market is divided into two primary types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves sensors that are installed directly in the environment where leakage is expected, allowing for precise monitoring of liquid presence. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection employs external sensors that detect leaks without physical penetration, relying on methods like acoustic or thermal sensing. Each type serves various applications, including industrial, commercial, and residential settings, tailored to specific leakage detection needs.</p></p>
