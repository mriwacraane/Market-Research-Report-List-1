<p><p>The solvent-based pressure label market encompasses various types, including release liner labels and linerless labels. Release liner labels utilize a backing material that separates from the adhesive until application, allowing for easy handling and precise placement. In contrast, linerless labels eliminate the need for a backing, enabling continuous labeling and reducing waste. Both types cater to different needs across industries, offering versatility for product labeling, branding, and packaging solutions while ensuring efficient application processes.</p></p>
