Sheets under 5mm are lightweight and often used for applications like window glazing and safety covers. The 5-10mm range offers improved durability and is suitable for signage and partitioning. Sheets thicker than 10mm provide high impact resistance and insulation, making them ideal for roofing, thermal barriers, and industrial applications. Each segment serves distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
