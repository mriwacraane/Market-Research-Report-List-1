<p><p>The 25G DFB laser diode chip market is divided into two primary types: single mode and multimode. Single mode lasers transmit light through a single optical path, ensuring minimal signal loss and higher performance over long distances, making them ideal for telecom applications. In contrast, multimode lasers support multiple light paths, enabling higher data transmission rates over shorter distances, which is beneficial for local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance, bandwidth, and installation requirements.</p></p>
