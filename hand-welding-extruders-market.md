<p><p>Hand welding extruders are crucial in joining plastic materials through specialized techniques. Contact welding involves directly heating the surfaces to be joined, allowing rapid bonding. This method is efficient for precise applications. In contrast, hot gas welding utilizes a stream of heated air or gas to melt the plastic, enabling the fusion of materials with greater flexibility in positioning. Both methods serve various industries, ensuring durable joints in plastic fabrication and repair processes.</p></p>
