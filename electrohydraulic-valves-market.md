<p><p>The electrohydraulic valves market includes various types showcasing distinct operational mechanisms. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a flapper mechanism to direct fluid flow through nozzles, allowing for precise control. Jet action valves employ jets of fluid to create movement and regulate pressure, offering rapid response times. Direct drive valves utilize electrical signals for immediate actuation, enhancing efficiency and reliability. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, capitalizing on their unique functional advantages.</p></p>
