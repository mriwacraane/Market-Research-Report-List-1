<p><strong>The Juice Extractors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Juicer</li><li>Masticating Juicer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The juice extractors market is categorized into three main types: centrifugal juicers, masticating juicers, and others. Centrifugal juicers use high-speed spinning to separate juice from pulp, ideal for quick juice extraction but may introduce more foam and heat. Masticating juicers operate at slower speeds, effectively squeezing fruits and vegetables for higher nutrient retention and less oxidation, making them suitable for leafy greens. The "others" category includes specialized juicers, such as citrus juicers and juice presses, catering to specific consumer needs.</p></p>
