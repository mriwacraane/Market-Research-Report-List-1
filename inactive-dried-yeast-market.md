<p><p>Inactive dried yeast, also known as dead yeast or deactivated yeast, is a non-leavening form of yeast that is heat-treated to deactivate its fermentation properties. It is widely used in the food and beverage industry as a flavor enhancer, nutritional supplement, and natural source of vitamins, minerals, and amino acids. Common applications include baked goods, snacks, soups, sauces, and animal feed. Its popularity stems from its ability to provide umami flavor, improve texture, and enhance nutritional profiles without affecting fermentation processes.</p><p>The Inactive Dried Yeast Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
