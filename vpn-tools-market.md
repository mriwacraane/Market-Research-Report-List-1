<p><p>The VPN tools market is primarily divided into two main types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN enables individual users to securely connect to a private network from a remote location, ideal for telecommuters and traveling professionals. In contrast, Site-to-Site VPN connects entire networks, allowing multiple sites or branch offices to communicate securely over the internet, facilitating inter-office collaboration. Both types enhance security and privacy for data transmission across potentially unsafe networks.</p></p>
