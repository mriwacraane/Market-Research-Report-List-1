<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial automatic coffee machine market consists of two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic machines handle all brewing processes, from grinding beans to frothing milk, offering convenience and consistency for high-demand settings. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual involvement, allowing skilled baristas to control brewing parameters while still automating aspects like grinding and steaming. Both types cater to different business needs, with fully automatic systems favoring efficiency and semi-automatic machines emphasizing barista craftsmanship.</p></p>
