<p><p>The multimode fiber splitter market is categorized into various types based on their configurations. The 1×2 splitter divides one input signal into two outputs, while the 1×4 and 1×8 splitters distribute an input signal to four and eight outputs, respectively. The 2×2 splitter serves as a dual input-output system, allowing flexibility in signal routing. Other types may include higher configurations or specialized designs for specific applications. These splitters are essential for efficiently managing fiber optic signals in communication networks.</p></p>
