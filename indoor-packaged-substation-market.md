<p><p>The indoor packaged substation market includes two primary types of transformers: liquid-filled transformers and dry type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers utilize insulation oil to cool and insulate, providing enhanced thermal management and efficiency, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. In contrast, dry type transformers use air as the cooling medium, offering safer operation without the risk of leaks, making them ideal for confined spaces and environments with stringent safety regulations. Both types cater to varying operational needs and installation conditions.</p></p>
