<p><p>Rod ends are mechanical components used to connect control rods and other elements in machinery. The market for rod ends can be categorized into internal threads and external threads. Internal thread rod ends feature a threaded hole for attachment to rods, providing a secure connection within the component. Conversely, external thread rod ends have a threaded exterior, allowing them to be affixed to other parts. This segmentation is crucial for industries requiring precise alignment and movement, such as automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
