In contrast, Infra-red Sensor Faucets utilize infrared technology to detect movement, automatically activating water flow when hands are detected nearby, promoting hygiene and water conservation. Both types enhance user experience in residential and commercial settings, catering to modern demands for efficiency and sanitation.</p></p>
