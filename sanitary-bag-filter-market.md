<p><p>The Sanitary Bag Filter market is segmented into three main types based on cleaning methods: Pulse Jet Cleaning, Reverse Air Cleaning, and Others. Pulse Jet Cleaning utilizes high-pressure bursts of air to dislodge trapped particles from the filter bags, ensuring consistent performance. Reverse Air Cleaning operates by reversing the airflow, allowing dust to fall into collection hoppers. The "Others" category includes alternative cleaning methods like shake or mechanical cleaning systems, catering to diverse industrial needs and ensuring effective filtration solutions.</p></p>
