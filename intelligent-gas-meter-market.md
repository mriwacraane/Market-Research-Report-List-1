<p><p>The intelligent gas meter market comprises several types, each suited to specific applications. Diaphragm meters are widely used for residential and light commercial use, offering accuracy in lower flow rates. Turbine meters are designed for high flow rates, ideal for industrial applications. Rotary piston meters are known for their reliability and precision, suitable for various gas types. Thermal mass flow meters measure gas flow based on thermal properties, while ultrasonic meters use sound waves for accurate measurements, often in larger systems.</p></p>
