<p><strong>The Inkjet Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Oil-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet inks market comprises three primary types: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, ideal for indoor applications, and widely used in consumer printers due to their quick drying times and vibrant colors. Oil-based inks offer durability and resistance to fading, making them suitable for signage and outdoor use. Solvent-based inks are versatile and ensure adhesion on various surfaces, commonly utilized in commercial applications and graphics printing, providing excellent outdoor performance and durability.</p></p>
