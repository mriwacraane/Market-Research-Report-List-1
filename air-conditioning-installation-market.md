<p><ul><li>Whole-Home</li><li>Split Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning installation market includes whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems provide centralized cooling, utilizing ductwork to ensure consistent temperature throughout the entire house, ideal for larger spaces. In contrast, split systems consist of an indoor unit and an outdoor compressor, offering targeted cooling for specific rooms or areas. This flexibility makes split systems suitable for smaller homes or those without existing ductwork. Both types cater to various customer preferences and space configurations, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.</p></p>
