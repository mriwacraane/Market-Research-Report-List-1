<p><p>The power splitter market encompasses various configurations designed to distribute signal power among multiple outputs. A 2-way splitter divides the input signal into two equal parts, commonly used in telecommunications. A 3-way splitter channels the signal into three outputs, offering versatility in setups. A 4-way splitter expands this further, allowing signal distribution to four devices. Other types may include higher splitters or specialized configurations for unique applications, catering to diverse industry needs, including broadband, cable TV, and satellite communications.</p></p>
