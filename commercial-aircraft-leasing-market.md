<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aircraft leasing market primarily consists of two types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves the rental of an aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance, allowing airlines to operate the aircraft independently. Wet leasing, on the other hand, includes the provision of an aircraft with crew, maintenance, and insurance, enabling airlines to immediately utilize the aircraft for operations. These leasing types cater to different operational needs, financial strategies, and regulatory requirements of airlines.</p></p>
