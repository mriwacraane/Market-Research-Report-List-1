Enterprise CTMS is designed for larger organizations, providing comprehensive solutions to manage the entire clinical trial process across multiple sites, enhancing collaboration and data integration. Both types aim to optimize clinical trial management, ensuring compliance and facilitating better outcomes.</p></p>
