<p><strong>The Industrial Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fiber laser market is primarily categorized into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant, uninterrupted beam, ideal for cutting and welding materials with high precision and speed, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers generate short, high-energy bursts of light, effective for applications requiring precise material removal and engraving, as well as for delicate processes in medical and electronics manufacturing. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in industrial applications.</p></p>
