<p><strong>The Excitation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Excitation Systems</li><li>Brushless Excitation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The excitation systems market is primarily categorized into static excitation systems and brushless excitation systems. Static excitation systems utilize thyristors and provide precise control of generator voltage, making them suitable for modern power applications with high reliability and quick response. In contrast, brushless excitation systems eliminate the need for physical brushes, employing rotating rectifiers for voltage regulation, which reduces maintenance requirements. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing generator stability and performance in power generation and industrial applications.</p></p>
