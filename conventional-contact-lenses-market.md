<p><p>The conventional contact lenses market is segmented into three main types: soft lenses, hard lenses, and rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses. Soft lenses are popular for their comfort and ease of use, made from flexible materials that conform to the eye. Hard lenses, though less common, provide durability and excellent vision correction, particularly for astigmatism. RGP lenses offer a hybrid option, combining durability and oxygen permeability, making them suitable for extended wear and vision correction while maintaining eye health.</p></p>
