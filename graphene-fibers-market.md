<p><p>The graphene fibers market is categorized into three main types based on material combinations. Polymer combined fibers integrate graphene with polymers to enhance strength and conductivity, making them ideal for textiles and composite applications. Inorganic metals combined fibers incorporate graphene with metallic elements, enhancing thermal and electrical properties, suitable for electronics and aerospace. Inorganic nonmetals combined fibers merge graphene with nonmetal elements, promoting lightweight and durable solutions, ideal for insulation and energy storage applications. Each type leverages graphene’s unique characteristics for improved performance.</p></p>
