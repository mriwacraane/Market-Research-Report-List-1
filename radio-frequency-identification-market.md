<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) market consists of three main types: tags, readers, and middleware. Tags are small devices attached to items, containing data that can be read wirelessly. Readers emit radio signals to capture this data from tags. Middleware serves as the software layer that processes the information collected by readers, integrating it with back-end systems for analysis and management. Together, these components enable efficient tracking and tracing of assets across various industries, enhancing operational efficiency and visibility.</p></p>
