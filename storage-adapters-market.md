<p><strong>The Storage Adapters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FC</li><li>FCoE</li><li>iSCSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The storage adapters market comprises various protocols for connecting storage devices to servers. Fibre Channel (FC) offers high-performance, low-latency connections, ideal for enterprise environments. Fibre Channel over Ethernet (FCoE) merges FC's reliability with Ethernet's flexibility, enabling efficient data center networking. Internet Small Computer Systems Interface (iSCSI) uses standard Internet protocols to connect storage devices over IP networks, providing cost-effective solutions for smaller businesses. Each type caters to specific performance, scalability, and cost needs in diverse IT infrastructures.</p></p>
