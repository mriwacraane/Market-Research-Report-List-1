AC controllers utilize alternating current, often favored for larger installations due to higher power capacity. DC controllers operate on direct current, providing more precision and efficiency, suitable for smaller applications. Each type caters to specific needs in agriculture, veterinary care, and animal husbandry, ensuring optimal temperature management for livestock and other animals.</p></p>
