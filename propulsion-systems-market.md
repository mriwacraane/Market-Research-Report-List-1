<p><ul><li>Air Breathing Engines</li><li>Non-Air Breathing Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propulsion systems market is divided into two main types: air-breathing engines and non-air-breathing engines. Air-breathing engines, such as turbojets and turbofans, utilize atmospheric oxygen for combustion, making them efficient for subsonic and supersonic flight. In contrast, non-air-breathing engines, like rockets, carry their own oxidizers and are suitable for space exploration and high-altitude applications. Both types are critical for various aerospace missions, offering distinct advantages based on operational environments and performance requirements.</p></p>
