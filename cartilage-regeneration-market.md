<p><p>The cartilage regeneration market encompasses various techniques for repairing damaged cartilage. Microfracture involves creating tiny holes in the bone to stimulate healing. Debridement removes damaged tissue to promote cartilage repair. Abrasion arthroplasty involves scraping the surface to facilitate regeneration. Autologous chondrocyte implantation uses the patient's cells for repair, while osteochondral autograft transplantation transfers healthy cartilage from another joint. Osteochondral allograft transplantation uses donor cartilage. Cell-based cartilage resurfacing employs stem cells to regenerate tissue. Other methods include novel techniques and emerging therapies.</p></p>
