<p><p>Solid State Lighting (SSL) encompasses various technologies, primarily LED, OLED, and PLED. LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes) are energy-efficient and long-lasting, widely used in general and specialty lighting. OLEDs (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) offer thin, flexible panels with superior color quality, ideal for displays and decorative lighting. PLEDs (Polymer Light Emitting Diodes) are a subset of OLEDs, using conductive polymers, enabling cost-effective manufacturing and lightweight applications. Together, these technologies are reshaping the lighting market with enhanced efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
